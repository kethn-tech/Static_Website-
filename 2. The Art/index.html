What Is Called Art?

Art, also called visual art, is a visual object or experience consciously created through an expression of skill or imagination. It speaks to our emotions and challenges our perspectives.

7 Types of Art

The seven different art forms are Painting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theater, Film, and Music. Each represents a unique way of human expression and creativity.

01

Painting

The art of applying pigment to a surface to create visual compositions that express ideas and emotions.

Oil Painting Watercolor Acrylic
02

Sculpture

Three-dimensional artworks created by shaping materials like stone, metal, wood, or clay.

Bronze Marble Modern Materials
03

Literature

Written works of artistic expression that explore human experience through language and narrative.

Poetry Novels Drama
04

Architecture

The art of designing and constructing buildings that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

Classical Modern Contemporary
05

Cinema

The art of creating moving images that tell stories and evoke emotions through visual narrative.

Direction Cinematography Editing
06

Music

The art of organizing sounds in time to create harmony, melody, and rhythm that speaks to the soul.

Classical Contemporary Electronic
07

Theater

Live performance art that combines acting, music, dance, and stagecraft to tell compelling stories.

Drama Musical Opera
